About us

Wel-come to Yatra

At Yatra, we're passionate about exploring the world, uncovering hidden gems, and sharing our love for travel with you. Whether you're an avid globetrotter or a first-time traveler, we're here to inspire your wanderlust and help you plan unforgettable journeys. We curate travel guides, tips, and resources to help you make the most of your journeys. We are committed to responsible and sustainable travel. We believe in respecting local cultures, preserving the environment, and supporting local communities. Our aim is to help you travel with a positive impact, leaving a destination better than you found it.

Discovering New Destinations

Manali

The town is a year-round destination, with visitors coming to experience the snowy winters and the pleasant summers. Manali is a haven for nature enthusiasts, adventure seekers, and travelers looking for a serene escape in the Himalayan foothills.

Rajasthan

Rajasthan's blend of history, culture, and natural beauty makes it a captivating destination for travelers looking to experience the essence of India's royal heritage and vibrant traditions.

Nagaland

Nagaland's combination of natural beauty, tribal traditions, and cultural vibrancy make it a unique and fascinating destination for travelers seeking an authentic and enriching experience in the northeastern part of India.
